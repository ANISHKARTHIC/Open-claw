# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to create a welcoming and inclusive environment.

## Our Standards
Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans may be applied.
